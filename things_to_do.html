Learn about the history!

tillamook rock lighthouse was constructed in 1881 to help sdailors navigate the dangerous tillamook head, a run so difficult sailor coined it "the terrible tilly". many belive the spirits of the sailors lost to it reside in the lighthouse, now trapped and unable to move on. learn about the deep and dark history surrounding the lighthouse and the many secrets it holds.
